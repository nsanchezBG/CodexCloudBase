Balance a moonlit market grid. Tap each lantern to set values 1–5, satisfy row and column totals, honor fixed givens, and use one district clue to finish the ledger.

How to play

Each square is a lantern worth 1–5. Click or tap to cycle values. The dark badges show row and column totals. Gold lanterns are fixed.

Start by matching the fixed lanterns, then make each row total agree with its badge.
